What are these settings?
The OT calculation depends on two key threshold times that define how your organisation counts duty hours. These values are optional — if you leave them blank, the system uses the built-in defaults (07:30 and 08:30). Only change these if your workplace has different official duty start times. After entering new values, press Apply to activate them.

• Morning OT Limit — If an employee arrives before this time, they are considered to be on morning overtime. The system will deduct 8 hours from their total duty duration to find the OT hours. Default: 07:30

• Normal Duty Start — This is the official start time of a normal working day. Employees who arrive between the Morning OT Limit and this time will have their duty counted from this time, not from when they actually arrived. The system deducts 7 h 45 m and requires a minimum of 1 hour OT to qualify. Default: 08:30
